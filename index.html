The RajaSaab (titled onscreen as Prabhas's The RajaSaab) is a 2026 Indian Telugu-language fantasy horror comedy film[7] written and directed by Maruthi, and produced by People Media Factory and IVY Entertainment.[8] The film stars Prabhas, alongside Sanjay Dutt, Nidhhi Agerwal, Malavika Mohanan (in her Telugu film debut), Riddhi Kumar and Zarina Wahab.[9][10] In the film, a man searching for his missing grandfather finds himself in a mansion inhabited by a sinister presence.
